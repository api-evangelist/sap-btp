There is a question being asked in boardrooms, team meetings, and coffee queues right now, and it is not going away: what is going to happen to my job? Artificial intelligence is moving faster than most organisations anticipated, and the honest answer is that nobody has the full picture yet. What we do know is that the way work gets done is changing — and that the organisations navigating this well are not the ones with the biggest AI budgets.
